Our Values - The Leeds Way

  • Patient-centred

  • Fair

  • Collaborative

  • Accountable

  • Empowered


Patient-centred

High quality, safe care organised around patients’ needs by compassionate staff.


Fair

We will treat others how we would wish to be treated

Strive to maintain the respect and dignity of each patient, being particularly attentive to the needs of vulnerable groups


Collaborative

Working with patients, their families and carers, and other NHS organisations to deliver joined-up care.


Accountable

Acting with honesty and integrity and accepting responsibility for our actions with patients, our communities and our colleagues.


Empowered

Helping people to make good decisions by strengthening morale, expertise and innovation.


Leeds Teaching Hospitals NHS Trust contribute to life in the Leeds region, not only by being one of the largest employers with more than 16,000 staff, but by supporting the health and well-being of the community and playing a leading role in research, education and innovation.
